# User Story: 06 - Achievement Badges

**As a** user,
**I want** to earn badges for reaching hydration milestones,
**so that** I feel rewarded and motivated to continue my healthy habits.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Users earn badges for achieving specific milestones (e.g., 7-day streak, 30-day streak, 100 glasses total)
* Earned badges are displayed on the dashboard
* Users receive visual feedback when a new badge is earned
* Badge collection persists across browser sessions
* Different badge types for different achievements (streak-based, total consumption, etc.)
* Users can view all earned badges and locked badges they haven't earned yet

## Notes

* Designer mentioned badges for milestones as a motivational feature
* Badges should be visually appealing and meaningful
* Consider progressive difficulty levels (bronze, silver, gold badges)
* Future features could include steps, sleep, and meditation badges
