# User Story: 08 - Leave and View Reviews

**As a** tenant who has stayed at a property,
**I want** to leave a review and rating for the accommodation,
**so that** I can help other users make informed decisions and provide feedback to landlords.

**As a** prospective tenant,
**I want** to read reviews from previous tenants,
**so that** I can feel more confident about my accommodation choice.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Review submission features:
  - Star rating (1-5 stars) for overall experience
  - Category ratings (cleanliness, location, value, communication)
  - Written review text (optional but encouraged)
  - Photo upload capability (optional)
  - Review can only be submitted after verified stay
* Review display on listing page shows:
  - Average overall rating prominently displayed
  - Total number of reviews
  - Breakdown of category ratings
  - Individual reviews with ratings, text, and photos
  - Reviewer name and stay date
  - Most recent reviews shown first (with option to sort)
* Reviews cannot be deleted, only responded to by landlord
* Moderation system to flag inappropriate reviews
* Landlords can respond to reviews

## Notes

* Review verification important to prevent fake reviews
* Only tenants who completed a stay can review
* Consider cooling-off period before review can be submitted
* Reviews build trust and help users make decisions
* Future enhancement: Review helpfulness voting
