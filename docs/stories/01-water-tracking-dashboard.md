# User Story: 01 - Water Tracking Dashboard

**As a** user,
**I want** to see a water tracking dashboard on the landing page with a visual counter,
**so that** I can easily track my daily water intake and stay motivated to reach my hydration goal.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Dashboard is displayed prominently on the landing page
* Water counter shows current glasses consumed vs daily goal (default 8 glasses)
* Visual representation makes it easy to see progress at a glance
* Counter updates in real-time when water intake is logged
* Dashboard has an intuitive, appealing design that motivates users

## Notes

* This is the priority #1 task per product owner and designer
* Should be simple and focused on water tracking only initially
* Default goal is 8 glasses per day
* Users should be able to customize the daily goal (future enhancement)
* Sets foundation for tracking other healthy daily routines (hydration, sleep, steps, meditation)
