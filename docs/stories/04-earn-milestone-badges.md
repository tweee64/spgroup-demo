# User Story: 4 - Earn Milestone Badges

**As a** user,
**I want** to earn badges when I reach meaningful milestones in my healthy habits,
**so that** I feel a sense of achievement and am encouraged to keep building good routines.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   Badges are awarded automatically when a user hits a defined milestone (e.g., 7-day streak, 30-day streak, first week of hitting the water goal).
*   Earned badges are displayed in the user's profile or a dedicated achievements section.
*   A notification or in-app celebration appears at the moment a badge is earned.
*   Unearned badges can be seen (greyed out) so users understand what they are working towards.
*   At least three milestone badges are defined for the initial release.

## Notes

*   Suggested by the Designer as a complement to the streaks feature for user motivation.
*   Specific badge names, artwork, and milestone thresholds are to be defined during backlog refinement.
